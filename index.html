FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internet services and packages.

What is the installation process?

Our installation process is quick and hassle-free. After you choose a package, our technician will schedule a convenient time to install the necessary equipment at your location. Installation typically takes 1-2 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards, mobile banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket), and bank transfers for your convenience.

Is there a contract or minimum term?

Our residential packages are available on a month-to-month basis with no long-term contract. Business packages may have different terms depending on the specific requirements.

What is your uptime guarantee?

We offer a 99.9% uptime guarantee for all our internet services. In the rare event of downtime, our team works around the clock to restore service as quickly as possible.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Yes, you can change your package at any time. Simply contact our customer support team, and they'll assist you with the transition to a package that better suits your needs.

Do you offer any discounts?

We occasionally run promotional offers and discounts for new customers. We also offer special rates for long-term prepayments and referrals. Contact us to learn about current offers.
